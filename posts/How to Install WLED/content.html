How to Install WLED on Your Device

WLED is a popular LED control software for controlling WS2812B and similar addressable RGB LED strips. In this guide, we will walk through how to install WLED on an ESP8266 or ESP32 device.

Materials Needed

- D1 Mini or ESP8266
- WS2812B LED Strip
- Power Supply for the LED Strip
- Micro-USB Cable
- WLED Firmware

Step 1: Install WLED Firmware

To install WLED on your D1 Mini or ESP8266, follow these steps:

Go to the official WLED Install website using Google Chrome or Chromium-based browsers.
Follow the instructions on the website.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hardware

After installing the WLED firmware, you need to connect your controlling board to the WS2812B LED strip.

Connect the VCC pin of the LED strip to the 5V pin of your microcontroller.
Connect the GND pin of the LED strip to the GND pin of your microcontroller.
Connect the DIN pin of the LED strip to the D4 pin of your microcontroller.

Step 3: Configure WLED

After installing the firmware and connecting the WS2812B LED strip,
connect to the web interface. The IP can be found in your WiFi router, and the device is named "WLED".

Then go to Config → LED Preferences, scroll to LED Outputs, and set the correct number of LEDs on your strip.

Afterward, enter the LED count in the "Length" field, scroll to the bottom, and click "Save".

Step 4: Enjoy Your Lights!

Once everything is set up, you can begin controlling your LED strip using the WLED interface.
